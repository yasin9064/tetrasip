
# Tetra Sip Website Blueprint

## Overview

This document outlines the design and development plan for the Tetra Sip website. Tetra Sip is a startup that provides customizable labels for water bottles. The website will serve as a marketing and sales platform for the company.

## Design and Style

The website will have a modern, clean, and visually appealing design.

*   **Color Palette:**
    *   Primary: Shades of blue (representing water)
    *   Secondary: White and light gray
    *   Accent: A vibrant color like turquoise or coral
*   **Typography:**
    *   Headings: A clean and modern sans-serif font (e.g., Montserrat, Lato)
    *   Body: A readable serif font (e.g., Lora, Merriweather)
*   **Imagery:** High-quality images of water bottles with custom labels, lifestyle shots, and abstract water-related graphics.
*   **Iconography:** Simple and modern icons to represent features and services.

## Features

*   **Hero Section:** A large, impactful hero section with a compelling headline and a call-to-action (CTA) button.
*   **About Us Section:** A section introducing Tetra Sip and its mission.
*   **Services Section:** A detailed overview of the customization options available.
*   **How It Works Section:** A step-by-step guide to ordering custom-labeled water bottles.
*   **Testimonials Section:** Customer reviews and testimonials.
*   **Contact Form:** A simple form for users to get in touch.
*   **Footer:** With links to social media and other relevant pages.
